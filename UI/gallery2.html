Amarkantak

Located in Madhya Pradesh

228 km from Jabalpur, a great pilgrim centre for the Hindus, Amarkantak is the source of rivers Narmada and Sone. While the rivers Narmada flows westwards, the Sone flow towards east from Amarkantak. Set in sylvan surroundings, it is situated at an altitude of 1065 m at the meeting point of the Vindhya and Satpura mountain ranges.

Digambara Ganesh Temple

Located in Madhya Pradesh

In Madhya Pradesh at Kadav there is a Digambar Siddhivinayaka. It is told Sage Kanva consecrated this idol. Since the idol is without clothes it is call a Digambara Ganesha.Kanva was a renowned rishi. He is the author of several hymns of the Rig Veda.
