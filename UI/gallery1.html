Sarbhanga Ashram

Located in Madhya Pradesh

The current location of this Ashram is around 13 kms from Chithara Station, Satna, Madhya Pradesh. The Sarabhanga Ashram is one of the first ashrams we find at the periphery of the Dandakaranya forest.

Sudekhsha Ashram

Located in Madhya Pradesh

This ashram is located deeper into the Dandakaranya forest and one has to go through rough forest terrain and small settlements to reach here.There is a huge open yard with trees all around and a small temple for Rama- Sita at one end

Agastya Ashram

Located in Madhya Pradesh

Agastya ashram is on top of a hill, a 16 kms trek from the point of nearest habitat, a small but very hospitable village called Tamouli in Panna district near Patna,Madhya Pradesh. The hill itself is on the banks of the small Tridthi river.

Sati Anusuya Ashram

Located In Madhya Pradesh

The Sati Anusuya Ashram is located at a distance of about 16 km from the town of Chitrakoot. It marks the place where they lived and meditated. It is set amidst dense forests.
